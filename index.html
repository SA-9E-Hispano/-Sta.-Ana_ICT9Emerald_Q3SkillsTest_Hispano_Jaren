Emergency Go Bag Essentials

Emergency Food and Water

Make sure to keep enough food and water to survive for at least a month.

Food and water is the most important thing to have for you to survive.

First Aid Kit

Keep a first aid kit to help injured closed ones.

In case someone in your family or know is injured, have a first aid kit ready to treat their injuries.

Flashlights and Safety Equipment

If it is dark, have a flashlight to know your surroundings.

Keep safety equipment like a whistle, ladder, rope etc. to make your survival chances higher.
